Hi, I'm Pouria!

I am a graduate BSc (4 years) with a major in Computer Engineering from Iran. I have worked on some research projects which some of them were under the supervision of prof. Shakeri as an undergraduate at the University of Guilan.

Also, I obtained my school's delegate to the Elementary Data Science Summer School (EDS3) at Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences(IPM), the most significant research institute in my country. I was among the top 100 out of 900 in competition from all around Iran to get into summer school and finally I got a white card which means that I was able to participate in school and final exam. Attending a data science school helped me learn machine learning at a higher level. The school's lessons included machine learning taught by Prof. Veisi from the University of Tehran, The Python for Data Science taught by Prof. Sadeghi from the University of Tehran, The Statistics for Data Science taught by Prof. Alishahi from the Sharif University of Technology and Fundamental of Data Science taught by Prof. Mazloom from the University of Amsterdam.

I code primarily for my research in Java and Python with different frameworks and libraries. In addition, I enjoy being challenged and engaging with projects that require me to work outside my comfort and knowledge set, as continuing to learn new languages and development techniques are important to me and the success of research.

I should mention that nothing is more amazing to me than listening to the advice of someone with over 30 years of research experience. I truly appreciate and value everything I have learned from Prof. Grogono for his generous advice on my resume.

Here is my interesting topics:

  • Software Engineering
  • Sentiment Analysis
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Optimization
  • Mobile Application
